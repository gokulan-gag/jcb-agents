#### UNDERSTAND THE NEED

- What will you primarily use the machine for? (landscaping, construction, utility work?)

- Do you need any specific attachments — bucket, forks, auger, or something else?

- How many hours per week do you expect to run it?

#### UNDERSTAND THEIR BUYING POSITION

- Are you replacing an existing machine or adding to your fleet?

- What's your timeframe — are you looking to buy in the next 30 days?

- Have you seen or operated a 3TS before, or would a demo be useful?

#### UNDERSTAND THE COMMERCIAL SITUATION

- Are you planning to purchase outright or would financing be of interest?

- Do you have an existing credit relationship with a lender?

_Get clarity on timeframe and financing early — it moves them from inquiry to committed buyer faster. Don't leave it to the end of the call._
